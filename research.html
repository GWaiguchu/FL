Research Highlights

Understanding the delicate relationship between human development and wildlife is at the heart of my research. Here are some of the key projects I’m currently working on:

  • Impact of Standard Gauge Railway on wildlife ecology: Examining how elephants utilize underpasses along the railway to mitigate habitat fragmentation.
  • Wildlife Mortality along major infrastructure: Studying seasonal patterns and dynamics of wildlife kills along the Mombasa-Nairobi Highway.
  • Bones as Ecological Indicators: Using wildlife bones to study mortality patterns and ecosystem health in Kenya’s National Parks.
